Barrel
You must not hit these with any beams of light or allow floating mines (see moving objects section) to hit these either. If you hit one you will have to restart the level.
Crossed beams are not considered merged

If you have two beams of light from different directions crossing on a square that square is not considered to be the merged color. In fact crossing is altogether useless since you are not allowed to make a coin spin with cross beams.
